The loop

Use every die. Hit every target.

1

Roll the dice

Every normal roll is checked by the game so it can be solved. You start at target 10.

2

Combine numbers

Use +, −, ×, and ÷. Division must be exact, and every original die must be used once.

3

Race to 100

Solve 10, 20, 30, and onward until the final target: 100.

Modes

Play sharp, daily, fast, steady, or calm.

Solo

Race from 10 to 100 with a 60-second target timer.

Daily

One scored attempt per day, shared puzzle, streaks, and a leaderboard.

Sprint

Push as far as you can in 60 seconds.

Climb

Clear levels, earn stars, and work through the map.

Relax

No timer, no pressure, just practice and better number sense.
